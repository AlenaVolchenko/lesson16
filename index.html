Round the world trip in Ukraine

You always want to visit interesting and unusual places, enjoying the color and originality of foreign landscapes. But, you see, in order to go on vacation abroad, it will take time, a mandatory package of documents and a certain amount of money, and combining these three points at the same time is not as often as we would like. But there is a great alternative to such a trip abroad, without leaving the borders of your native country. It is not surprising, but there are many places on the territory of Ukraine, having visited which experienced travelers do not immediately remember that they are not in Africa, Italy, the Netherlands, Germany, Great Britain or another no less interesting country.

COLORFUL LITTLE "VENICE" IN ODESSA REGION

300 km from the amazing and cheerful Odessa, almost on the very border with Romania, there is a small town of Vilkovo. It would seem that there is nothing surprising in this town, as in many others, but no, Vilkovo is a city on the water, like Venice. Here, instead of the usual sidewalks and streets, there are canals along which local residents travel in Ukrainian variations of gondolas. The air here is unusually clean and fresh, the landscapes are amazingly beautiful and romantic.

CORNER OF JAPAN IN UZHGOROD

Japan has always attracted with its culture, architecture, achievements and unique nature, and the flowering of Japanese sakura leaves no one indifferent. You will be surprised, but in order to enjoy the beautiful alley of Japanese cherry blossoms, it is not at all necessary to fly to the Land of the Rising Sun; all this beauty is available in the colorful Ukrainian city of Uzhgorod. Therefore, we advise you to spend the May weekend, even next year, here, plunging into the fabulous atmosphere of a flowering alley, showering it with a rain of delicate rose petals. Well, if you are not too lazy and go a little deeper into the city to Dovzhenka and Rakoczi streets, then you can admire another blooming miracle - white and purple magnolias.

As you can see, our country, with all its routine and, it would seem, familiarity, keeps many beautiful places and mysteries that can surprise, fall in love and attract again and again. So there is no need to rotate the globe or study the map in search of a successful tourist route, just pack your suitcase or backpack, buy a ticket at the nearest train station and go to meet adventures and scenic views, being sure that even a very short period of time will be enough for a great vacation.
